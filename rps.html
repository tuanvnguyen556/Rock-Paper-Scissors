The Pokemon Foundation

Welcome to the Pokémon Foundation, where our passion for Pokémon knows no bounds! We are a dedicated team of trainers, volunteers, and enthusiasts who have come together to further connect children and their passion with Pokemon card trading. Our mission is to spread the joys of Pokémon with children all across the world, support children with collecting their favorite pokemon cards, and promote this safe community for children. Without your kind donations and immense support, we would not be able to set up these amazing events!

Events

Children can bring their Pokemon card collections to the event and participate in card trading sessions. This allows them to interact with other kids, build their decks, and learn strategy. Organizing friendly Pokemon card battles is also a popular activity where children can test their skills against one another.

A Pokemon-themed scavenger hunt can be a thrilling activity for children. Participants are given a list of Pokemon to find hidden around the event venue. Each Pokemon can be represented by a picture or a small figurine. When a child finds one, they can mark it off their list. Prizes or small rewards can be given to those who complete the hunt.

Creative activities like Pokemon-themed arts and crafts workshops excite children. They can make their own Pokemon masks, draw Pokemon characters, or paint rocks to resemble their favorite Pokemon. These workshops allow kids to express their creativity while staying true to the theme.
